OWEN EDDY

Composer

⌘
STUDIO, CONCERT, FILM

Owen Eddy is a composer, arranger, and audio engineer specializing in new music. His music has been described as having a “refreshing aura of optimism and openness” (Michael Parloff, Retired Principal Flutist, Metropolitan Opera Orchestra) as well as “attention-grabbing” and “clever” (Wolfram Koessel, Cellist, American String Quartet)
